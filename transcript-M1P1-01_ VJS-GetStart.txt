VIDEO TRANSCRIPT: Virtual Job Shadow: Getting Started

Virtual Job Shadow is a career planning platform that allows you to browse and explore many careers. 

To log in to for the first time, select New Account in the upper right-hand corner. 

Register for a new account using your Davidson-Davie email address as your username and you will 
then create a password of your choosing. Your College email address can be located on your admission 
letter from the College.

Once you’re logged in, you’re ready for the next step!

Next, we’ll visit an online Interest Profiler in Virtual Job Shadow to learn your three-letter 
Holland Code.
